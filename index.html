Hi. I'm Gerilyn.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and educational copywriting. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting my nose stuck in my latest good read, I can be found writing tech blog posts, creating music and enjoying quality time with my family.

I was born and raised in Galveston, Texas where my primary creative outlet was writing short stories for my middle and high school English classes. I've moved into narrative non-fiction these days.

My passion: I LOVE music – so much, in fact, that I formed a hardcore punk band with my dear friends. We love playing local shows although we've had to cancel some due to COVID-19.

A fun fact:My ears perk up anytime I hear someone mention these two things: The Office (UK & US) and true crime podcasts. It's a little eclectic, I know. But I love them both!
